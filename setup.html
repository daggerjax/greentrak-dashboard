GreenTrak Setup Guide

Track your portfolio in one place. Follow these steps to get started.

6 Steps total
~15 Minutes
0 Coding required
1

Copy the Template Sheet

1 minute

Click the link below. Google will ask if you want to make a copy. Click "Make a copy."

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1fMWVowm-nIZ3cW7S7JhjA0ao18DtWtuUNfQ0gmxM7Vs/copy

This gives you your own private Google Sheet with everything pre-configured. Your data stays in your Google account. Nobody else can see it.

2

Get Your Sheet ID

30 seconds

Open your new copy. Look at the URL in your browser's address bar. It looks like this:

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/abc123xyz_YOUR_ID_HERE/edit

The part between /d/ and /edit is your Sheet ID. Select it and copy it. You'll need it in the next step.

3

Connect the Script

3 minutes

This is the longest step, but you only do it once.

3a. In your copied sheet, click Extensions in the top menu bar, then click Apps Script. A new tab opens with code in it.

3b. Near the top of the code, find the line that says:
SHEET_ID: "YOUR_SHEET_ID_HERE"

3c. Replace YOUR_SHEET_ID_HERE with the Sheet ID you copied in Step 2. Keep the quotes around it.

3d. Press Cmd+S (Mac) or Ctrl+S (PC) to save.
4

Deploy the Script

2 minutes

Still in the Apps Script tab:

4a. Click the blue Deploy button (top right), then New deployment.

4b. Click the gear icon next to "Select type" and choose Web app.

4c. Under "Execute as," select Me.

4d. Under "Who has access," select Anyone.

4e. Click Deploy.

4f. Google will ask you to authorize. Click Authorize access, pick your Google account, and click Allow.

4g. You'll see a URL. Copy this URL. This is your personal script URL. Save it somewhere safe.

Note: If you see "Google hasn't verified this app," click "Advanced" then "Go to (project name) (unsafe)." This is normal for personal scripts.

5

Open the Dashboard

1 minute

Go to the dashboard:

https://daggerjax.github.io/greentrak-dashboard/
5a. You'll see a setup screen. Paste your script URL into the "Data URL" field.

5b. Leave "Parse URL" blank.

5c. Click Save & Load.

The dashboard will load. It will be empty because you haven't imported any positions yet. That's next.

6

Import Your Positions

5 minutes

This is what you'll do each month to update your portfolio.

6a. Log into each brokerage account and download your positions as a CSV file.

6b. Go to Google Drive and create a folder called exactly: GreenTrak Imports

6c. Upload all your CSV files into that folder.

6d. Go back to the dashboard and click the Parse button.

6e. Wait a few seconds. Your positions will appear.

That's it! The dashboard will show your combined portfolio with live prices, daily changes, and benchmarks.

Common Questions

Can anyone see my portfolio?

No. Your data lives in your personal Google Sheet. The dashboard runs entirely in your browser. Your script URL is stored only on your device. Nobody else can see your data, not even the person who shared this with you.

How do I update my portfolio each month?

Download fresh CSV files from your brokerage accounts, upload them to your "GreenTrak Imports" folder in Google Drive (you can replace the old ones or leave them, the system only processes the most recent batch), and click Parse on the dashboard.

What if I get a "Google hasn't verified this app" warning?

This is normal. Google shows this for any personal Apps Script. Click "Advanced" then "Go to (project name) (unsafe)." It's your own script running on your own account.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes. Open the dashboard URL in your phone's browser. It works on any device. Bookmark it for quick access.

What if I switch browsers or clear my data?

You'll see the setup screen again. Just paste your script URL back in. Your portfolio data is safe in your Google Sheet, nothing is lost.

The Parse button says "network error." What do I do?

Make sure your "GreenTrak Imports" folder exists in Google Drive and has CSV files in it. If it still fails, open the dashboard settings (gear icon), verify your URL is correct, and try again.
